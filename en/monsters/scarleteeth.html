A Littlered that turns feral can grow into this form: the Scarleteeth, a hulking clawed beast with a taste for flesh. For a person to take this form in battle is to lose oneself to pure animal bloodlust.

“Little Red Riding Hood” has many variations – the most popular being the Brothers Grimm version of the story. Some early versions explicitly describe the wolf in the story as a werewolf.
