Fusion

“Fusion” is a rare phenomenon known by the rangers. There are fused monsters in the wild, and supposedly the leader of the rangers can do it too. This is it. This is the rogue fusion! I hope you're ready for a tough fight, {player}. When your goals are aligned with those of your companion, you can fuse to become a singular, more powerful beast.\n\nThe closer you are to your companion, the more powerful you are as a fusion.\n\nKeep an eye on your fusion meter, though! You can only fuse when this is full.
